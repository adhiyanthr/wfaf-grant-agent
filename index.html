GrantEquity is a nonprofit infrastructure project. We automate the grant discovery work that small organizations can't afford to staff — so they can focus on the work that actually matters.

The problem

Why small nonprofits miss grants.

1 million+

U.S. nonprofits operate on budgets under $50,000 — too small for paid grant tools, too busy to search manually.

Sources: Urban Institute, National Center for Charitable Statistics, 2025; IRS Exempt Organizations Data, 2024

Grant discovery tools can cost up to $4,000 a year, and still require staff to manage them. Most small nonprofits have neither the budget nor the bandwidth. GrantEquity has no paid tier or paywall — a weekly email delivers your matches, and a free app lets you review fit scores and reasoning for each one.

How it works

Set it up once. It runs every week.

1

Tell us about your org

Your name, focus areas, county, and what you do. Two minutes, no account.

2

We search every Monday

We check foundation pages, county programs, and state portals for grants your org qualifies for that week.

3

You get an email

A short list of matching grants with amounts, deadlines, and links. Open the ones that fit and apply.

4

Review your matches

Log in to see fit scores, eligibility flags, and your agent's reasoning for each grant. Mark what you'll apply to.

In the app

What you'll see in your matches dashboard.

Fit score

A 0–100 score showing how well each grant matches your org's focus, size, and location.

Eligibility flags

Key requirements surfaced at a glance: budget caps, 501(c)(3) required, geographic limits.

Agent reasoning

A short explanation from the AI of why this grant is or isn't a match for you.
